# Remote Astronomy Charity

This project supports **open-access astronomy education** and the development of a
**remote-controlled telescope observatory** for outreach and instruction.

The repository contains:

- Design plans and automation code for a roll-off roof observatory  
- Free astronomy worksheets and teaching materials  
- Open-source software for remote telescope control  

---

## Contents

- **[Observatory Design](plans/)**  
  Structural plans, pier design, roof automation, and safety systems.

- **[Course Materials](course/)**  
  Worksheets, lessons, and outreach material (hosted on Google Drive).

- **[Code & Automation](code/)**  
  Scripts, control software, and experimental APIs.

---

## Mission

The goal is to lower barriers to astronomy education by combining
**open hardware**, **open software**, and **freely accessible learning materials**.
